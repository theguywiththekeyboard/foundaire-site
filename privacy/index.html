Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 28, 2025

Foundaire (“we,” “us,” or “our”) respects your privacy. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. It applies to foundaire.com and any pages we control that link to it.

What we collect

  • Information you submit: name, email, company, website, and your message via our contact form; scheduling details you provide when booking time with us.
  • Technical basics: standard web logs (e.g., IP address, browser type, timestamps) from our hosting provider for security and reliability.
  • No behavioral ads: we don’t sell personal data or run interest-based advertising.

How we use information

  • To reply to inquiries, schedule calls, and deliver services you request.
  • To secure, maintain, and improve our website and communications.
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms.

Our service providers (processors)

We use trusted vendors to operate our site and communications. They process your data only on our instructions:

  • Hosting: Vercel, Inc. (site delivery and logs).
  • Contact form: Formspree, Inc. (message relay and basic spam prevention).
  • Email & scheduling: Microsoft 365 / Microsoft Bookings (email, calendar, and booking pages hosted at outlook.office365.com).

We may add or change vendors over time; material changes will be reflected here.

Legal bases (if you’re in the EEA/UK)

  • Contract (responding to your request / booking and providing services).
  • Legitimate interests (site security, preventing abuse, improving our site).
  • Consent where required (e.g., optional marketing emails).

Retention

We keep contact submissions and related correspondence for up to 18 months unless we need them longer for an active project or legal reasons. Booking metadata is kept per Microsoft’s defaults. We retain minimal security logs per our host’s standard rotation.

Your choices & rights

  • Access, correction, deletion: Email hello@foundaire.com and we’ll help.
  • Marketing: We send marketing only with consent; you can opt out any time.
  • Cookies: We currently do not use non-essential tracking cookies. If this changes, we’ll request consent (where required) before setting them.

Security

We use HTTPS, modern security headers, and reputable providers. No method is 100% secure; we encourage you not to include sensitive information in free-text fields.

International transfers

Our providers may process data in the United States and other jurisdictions. We use vendors that commit to appropriate safeguards.

Children

Our site is not directed to children under 13, and we don’t knowingly collect their data.

Third-party links

Our site may link to external services (e.g., Microsoft Bookings). Their privacy practices apply to your use of those services.
